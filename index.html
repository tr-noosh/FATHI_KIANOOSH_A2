Welcome to Dunkeltaler Forest, home of the Alpen Signal Observatorium!

Dunkeltaler Forest is a center for space research, as well as a national park! There are many things to do at any time of the year. Visit the research base for a guided tour and live demonstration, or enjoy the many picnic and camping areas.

Dunkeltaler Forest (meaning "Dark Valley" Forest) is a big space used for space signal research located in the Swiss Alps. The river runs all the way the entire park, and there are trails all around the park to take. The location was chosen for it's geographical advantages and distance from light polluters, so those staying at night can enjoy a bright night sky full of stars!
